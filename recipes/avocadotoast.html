Avocado Toast

Description

Get ready for brunch! This is a Sunday morning classic - avocado toast is the perfect way to start your day. Creamy, ripe avocado is spread onto a bread of your choice, and from there it is anyone's call what to top it with! Eggs, cottage cheese, fresh tomatoes, onions, the list goes on.... a dish you can truly make your own. My favorite preparation is on toasted whole wheat bread, 2 eggs on each slice, topped with salt & pepper and a little pico de gallo. Simple tex-mex style!

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • Fresh, ripe avocados - sliced
  • Pico de gallo salsa - homemade or storebought
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Wash, decore, peel, and slice avocado.
  3. Heat butter or oil in a skillet, letting it heat for 1-2 min before adding eggs.
  4. Add eggs and pan fry, 3-4min on both sides.
  5. Season your eggs with salt and pepper, if desired.
  6. Once bread is finished toasting, take sliced avocado and mash lightly with a fork to create a spread.
  7. Add cooked eggs on top, season once more.
  8. Top with pico de gallo and serve.
