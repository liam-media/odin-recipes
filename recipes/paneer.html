Description

A delicious amalgam of flavors, the Paneer Rice is a Portuguese - Indian fusion dish featuring Indian cottage cheese, rice, and garlic aioli. Complete with a toasted pita for a scrumptious on-the-go wrap option! The garlic aioli gives it a zippy, creamy flavor that cannot be denied. It can be found at restaurants such as The Port of Peri Peri, quickly expanding across the globe.

Ingredients

  • Portuguese rice
  • Minced garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Sliced paneer
  • Heavy cream
