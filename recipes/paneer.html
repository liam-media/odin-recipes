Paneer Rice

Home

Description

A delicious amalgam of flavors, the Paneer Rice is a Portuguese - Indian fusion dish featuring Indian cottage cheese, rice, and garlic aioli served over a bed of tender Portuguese rice with spices and chiles. Complete with a toasted pita for a scrumptious on-the-go wrap option! The garlic aioli gives it a zippy, creamy flavor that cannot be denied. It can be found at restaurants such as The Port of Peri Peri, quickly expanding across the globe.

Ingredients

  • Portuguese rice
  • Minced garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Sliced paneer
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Cook portuguese rice in a rice cooker or sauce pot once boiling.
  2. Slice paneer block into strips
  3. Heat up a drizzle of your cooking oil of choice in a fry pan for 1-2 min.
  4. Begin searing the paneer until it develops grill marks on both sides, 4-5 min each side.
  5. In a small dish, add the minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and mayonnaise together. Stir / whip until homogenous.
  6. Combine ingredients and serve.
